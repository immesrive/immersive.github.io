What is this project about?

This is a prototype of a genre in which I am making a project in - virtual tryon. This market has been rapidly expanding in the upcoming years, and I decided to make a project because of this.

Virtual Tryon?

Virtual Tryon is the use of devices for testing and trying out wearables via the device(and not in-person) before buying.

You can see three buttons in the home screen. The first one stops/pauses video, the second one starts/resumes video, and the third resets Orbit Controls(thing you can use to move the screen and see how things look like in side view).

Make sure that your face can be seen clearly, be, ideally 1-2 feet from the camera, and click the second button

Wait for the program to load.

When the loader stops, you should see a "mask" almost on your face

On the right of the program you will see a black bar with many folders each having options. Click these olders

PLAY AROUND! Check out the settings. Reload the page to reset them.
